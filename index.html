Naruto Uzumaki

Naruto Uzumaki is the main protagonist of the Naruto series. Known for his determination, ninja skills, and heart, Naruto dreams of becoming Hokage and bringing peace to the ninja world.

Sasuke Uchiha

Sasuke Uchiha is Naruto's rival and a powerful ninja from the Uchiha clan. He seeks revenge on his brother Itachi and struggles with his own path in the world.

Sakura Haruno

Sakura Haruno is a skilled medical ninja and part of Team 7 with Naruto and Sasuke. Known for her intelligence and strength, she is a loyal friend and formidable fighter.
